# Bad Message Examples

## Bad example 1
Dear Hiring Manager, I am writing to express my sincere interest in this excellent opportunity and have attached my PDF resume for your kind consideration.

Problems:
1. Sounds like email, not chat.
2. Too formal.
3. Mentions PDF specifically.

## Bad example 2
Hi, I only work on C2C. Please confirm if your W2 role can be changed.

Problems:
1. Rigid and premature.
2. Makes work type the whole message.
3. Ignores technical fit.
