# Subject Patterns

Preferred subject styles:

1. `{Job Title} - Pranay`
2. `{Company} {Job Title} - Resume attached`
3. `{Job Title} | Python/AI delivery fit`
4. `{Job Title} | Relevant contract/project experience`

Rules:

1. Keep under 70 characters when practical.
2. Avoid generic subjects such as:
   - "Job Application"
   - "Interested Candidate"
   - "Regarding the opening"
3. If the company name is unknown, omit it rather than inventing one.
4. If the role is `okayish`, do not oversignal certainty in the subject.
