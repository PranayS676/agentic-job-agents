# Research Methodology
1. Read the full job description, not only the short summary.
2. Compare all available resume tracks before selecting one.
3. Prefer the track with the strongest evidence density for the target role.
4. Prioritize explicit JD requirements over inferred nice-to-haves.
5. Keep edits constrained to summary, skills, and one selected experience section.
6. Treat exact cloud/platform requirements as real gaps if the evidence is missing.
7. Only suggest additions that are defensible from the selected track or clearly adjacent evidence.
8. If the job is only okayish, keep the plan narrower than for a strong fit.
9. ATS estimates must be plausible; do not inflate them.
10. Every change recommendation must name a section, an action, and a JD-grounded reason.
