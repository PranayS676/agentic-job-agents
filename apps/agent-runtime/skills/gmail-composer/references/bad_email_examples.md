# Bad Email Examples

## Bad example 1
Subject: Job Application

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my sincere interest in your esteemed organization. I believe my vast experience and expertise make me the perfect candidate for this role.

Please find attached my PDF resume for your review and favorable consideration.

Problems:
1. Generic and inflated.
2. Uses unsupported "perfect candidate" language.
3. Mentions PDF specifically.
4. Sounds like a template, not a recruiter-facing note.

## Bad example 2
Subject: Immediate Joiner C2C Only

Hi,

I only work on C2C. Please confirm whether your W2 role can be converted.

Problems:
1. Overly rigid.
2. Turns the message into a demand instead of a fit discussion.
3. Conflicts with role-specific messaging guidance for W2 posts.
