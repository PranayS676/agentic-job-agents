# Tone Rules

1. Keep the message tight enough to read comfortably in chat.
2. Use a direct, professional tone with no email-style padding.
3. Mention only one concrete fit signal.
4. Do not oversell or invent experience.
5. For `okayish` or `draft` cases, sound exploratory rather than certain.
6. Avoid long greetings, signatures, and paragraph-heavy formatting.
